A Guide to Brampton

The Flower City blooming with growth!

Dress for the Weather!

Brampton has cold winters and hot summers, it's always important to pay attention to the weather forcast on a daily basis. It could be snowing one day like it is the middle of December and warm the next day like the middle of May!

Go car-free

It's not just an environmental statement, it's a smart choice. Brampton has a limited amount of parking spaces, and the infrastucture in some areas simply does not support the large population. Walk or ride your bike to nearby areas whenever you can!

Taking ZUM

ZUM is Brampton's Public Transit and offers many different rides across the city on major roads such as Bovaird Drive, Queens Street, Main Steet, Hurontario Road, and more. Download the offical ZUM app to learn about specific bus timings and trackers.

Thank You!

Always be sure to follow any specific parking policies given by administration of certain locations. This app is meant to direct you to the most efficient parking spaces to avoid traffic and free up space for essential travel.
